A message from the CEO to the World

Why This Foundation Was Established

Mother Evelyn T. Desuah — Founder & CEO

siahkorha@gmail.com +1 (510) 437-0039

"The world does not lack resources — it often lacks compassion, unity, and people willing to act."

"Real charity is not about creating dependence; it is about helping people discover hope, confidence, and the opportunity to build a better future."

"Love expressed through action has the power to transform lives."

"To everyone around the world — my name is not the most important part of this story. The most important part is the lives of the children, families, and communities whose voices are too often unheard."

My name is Mother Evelyn T. Desuah. I stand before you today not simply as the CEO of a charity organization, but as someone whose heart has been shaped by the pain, struggles, and resilience of people living in poverty.

I have seen children whose only meal for the day was uncertain. I have met young boys and girls who dreamed of going to school but could not because their families could not afford books, uniforms, or school fees. I have held the hands of elderly people who had no one left to care for them. I have listened to mothers who cried silently because they could not provide food or medicine for their children.

Those moments changed my life forever.

I realized that the world does not lack resources — it often lacks compassion, unity, and people willing to act. I understood that if those of us who have the ability to help remain silent, then we become part of the problem. That realization became the beginning of this organization.

This charity was not established to build a name or to seek recognition. It was established because every child deserves the opportunity to grow in safety, receive an education, and dream without fear. It was established because no family should be abandoned during difficult times. It was established because we believe that every human life has equal value, regardless of where they were born or how much they own.

Our mission goes beyond giving food or clothing.

While those needs are important, we also believe in restoring dignity, creating opportunities, supporting education, strengthening families, improving health, and empowering communities to become self-reliant. Real charity is not about creating dependence; it is about helping people discover hope, confidence, and the opportunity to build a better future.

The journey has not been easy. There have been moments when our resources were limited and our challenges seemed impossible. We have faced disappointment, rejection, and financial struggles. There were days when we wondered how we would continue serving those who depended on us.

Yet every time we considered giving up, something extraordinary happened. A volunteer offered their time. A donor shared what they could. A community came together. A child smiled after receiving help. A family found hope again.

Those moments reminded us that even the smallest act of kindness can create lasting change.

Today, our work is driven by one simple belief: love expressed through action has the power to transform lives. Every child we educate, every family we support, every community we strengthen, and every life we uplift becomes proof that compassion is stronger than despair.

To governments, businesses, faith communities, philanthropists, and people of goodwill across the world — I invite you to stand with us.

You do not have to be wealthy to make a difference. You only need a willing heart. Your knowledge, your time, your encouragement, your prayers, your partnerships, and your generosity can help create opportunities for those who need them most.

To every child who feels forgotten — we see you. To every parent carrying heavy burdens — we stand with you. To every young person who believes your future has been stolen by poverty or hardship — never lose hope. Your circumstances do not define your destiny.

As CEO, I measure success not by awards or recognition, but by the number of lives that are transformed. Success is seeing children return to school, families regain stability, communities become stronger, and hope replace despair.

"This organization exists because we believe that kindness can change the world one life at a time. Our commitment is to serve with integrity, transparency, compassion, and respect."

We will continue working until every child has a chance to thrive, every vulnerable family has support, and every community has the opportunity to build a brighter future.

This is more than an organization. It is a movement of hope. It is a promise that no one should be forgotten simply because they are poor, vulnerable, or powerless.

Together, we can build a world where compassion is stronger than indifference, where generosity overcomes poverty, and where every child grows up knowing they are loved, valued, and full of potential.

Thank you for believing that hope is worth investing in. Thank you for joining us in changing lives. Together, we can leave a legacy that future generations will be proud of.

Mother Evelyn T. Desuah Founder & CEO — Mother Evelyn Child-Care Foundation
