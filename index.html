Our Framework

Care. Educate. Empower. Transform.

Four words from our seal guide everything we do — and every program we run falls under one of them.

Care

Direct support for children and families who need it most right now.

  • Child care & welfare
  • Healthcare & well-being
  • Relief & humanitarian aid

Educate

Building the schools, supplies and mentorship that open doors for life.

  • School supplies & materials
  • Mentoring & counseling
  • Schools, vocational & training centers

Empower

Skills, leadership and confidence for the youth and women shaping tomorrow.

  • Youth & women's empowerment
  • Leadership development
  • Community development

Transform

Play, partnership and sustainable giving that turn support into lasting change.

  • Sports & recreation
  • Fundraising & partnerships
  • Student exchange programs

Our Mission

Protecting, nurturing and empowering every vulnerable child.

We work to protect, nurture, and empower vulnerable children by providing access to quality education, healthcare, nutrition, shelter, and emotional support — creating a safe, caring environment where every child is valued and given the chance to build confidence and reach their full potential.

Through partnerships with families, communities, volunteers, and supporters, we work to break the cycle of poverty and neglect with sustainable programs rooted in learning, growth, and inclusion.

Our Vision

A Liberia where every child grows up safe, loved and given a fair start.

We envision communities where children are protected from neglect, abuse, poverty, and discrimination — and are empowered to grow into confident, healthy, and responsible individuals who give back to the world around them.
